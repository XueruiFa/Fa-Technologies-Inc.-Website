WELCOME TO MY COMPANY

My Company is the premier company at the University of Pennsylvania School of Engineering and Applied Sciences. We are the primary source for students interested in My Company to gain professional experience and access to resources within the Company.

ABOUT
My Company is the 2016 Platinum chapter of the American Best Company Association at the University of Pennsylvania. It was founded in 2006 by the famous scientists on the right, Albert Einstein and Richard Feynman, in order to be the primary source for students interested in my company to gain professional experience and access to resources within the industry. Today, My Company engages hundreds of students across campus, with a 1500 person listserv, and remains the University of Pennsylvania's premier company club. Despite My Company's massive growth, it continues to provide individuals with unique and exciting opportunities in the field of companies through engaging events and resources.
OUR MISSION
To promote the academic study and professional practice of companies among University of Pennsylvania students by providing a forum for students, field experts and community leaders to share their knowledge and creativity. In other words, we do all things in My Company, here at Penn.
