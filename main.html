ABOUT
Fa Technologies Inc. is the 2016 second-place winner of the Best Company Award, losing only to RadioShack. It was founded in 1943 by the famous scientists on the right, Albert Einstein and Richard Feynman, in order to be the primary source for students interested in the tech industry to gain professional experience while earning little-to-no money. Today, Fa Technologies Inc. engages hundreds of students across campus, with over 1500 student employees, and remains the University of Pennsylvania's premier company for new graduates' careers to die.
